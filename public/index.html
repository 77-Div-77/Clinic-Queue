How It Works

Three steps to a smarter clinic

From chaos to clarity in minutes. No hardware. No app installs. Just open a browser.

1

Receptionist adds patient

Type a name, hit Enter. ClinicQ assigns the next token automatically and adds the patient to the live queue — in under 5 seconds.

→
2

Doctor calls the next token

One click on "Call Next" advances the queue. The timer starts, wait times recalculate across every connected screen — instantly.

→
3

Patients see it live on their phone

The patient screen updates automatically. No refresh. No asking "how long?". Enter your token to see your exact position and real wait time.

Receptionist Screen

Zero training needed. Under 5 seconds per patient.

Designed for speed. Every element exists for one reason: to get the next patient into the queue as fast as possible — without mistakes.

⚡

One-field, Enter-key form

Only the patient's name is required. Press Enter — done. Token assigned, queue updated, input cleared and re-focused automatically.

📊

Live stats bar

Patients waiting, total served today, real avg consultation time, and number of data samples — all updating in real-time.

↩

30-second undo window

Called the wrong token? Hit Undo within 30 seconds. The queue reverts completely — no data lost, no patient confused.

⏱

Live consultation timer

Shows remaining time for the current patient. Turns red when overrunning. Lets the receptionist know when to expect the next call.

Patient Screen

Patients know exactly where they stand. Always.

The patient-facing screen works on any phone browser. No app. No login. Open the link, see the queue update live — it's that simple.

🔢

Giant animated token display

The "Now Serving" number is the entire screen. When it changes, it flips with a 3D animation — unmissable from across the room.

🔍

Self-service token lookup

Patients type their own token number to instantly see their position, tokens ahead, and a real estimated wait time — no receptionist needed.

🔔

"Your turn soon" alert

When a patient is 1 token away, their screen shows a full-screen "Get Ready!" alert. No need to watch the display constantly.

📶

Auto-reconnect on network drop

Socket.IO reconnects automatically. When the connection restores, the screen receives the full current state within milliseconds.
